1. The player must be allowed to quit the game at any time. There has to be a button for that.
2. The current amount of gold in the treasury has to be shown to the player at all times next to the amount that has to be paid during the next winter.
3. If the player chooses to train armies in the region, then the amount of armies trained per turn has to be shown to the player. If it takes several turns to train an army, then show the amount of turns left.
4. Culture upgrade progress needs to be shown to the player.
5. Building construction progress needs to be shown to the player.
6. In case the user produces diplomats, the predicted loyalty change needs to be shown to the player.
7. After the player chooses to move armies from 1 region to another, there must be an arrow visible between the 2 regions. Clicking on the arrow shows a dialog which asks if the player wants to cancel the movement order? Clicking yes will cancel it and close the dialog, clicking no will close the dialog and the movement order stays as it was. Arrows are only clickable before turn submission.
8. If the player chooses to collect gold in a region, then the amount of gold collected per turn needs to be shown to the player.
